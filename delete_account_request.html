Account Deletion Request – GroveHR

This page is intended for users of the GroveHR app, developed by ri-square infoserve, who wish to delete their account and associated data.

How to Request Deletion

Currently, account deletion is not available directly within the app. To request deletion of your account, please send an email to:

Email: risquareinfoserve@gmail.com

In your request, include the following information:

  • Your registered email address
  • Your full name (used in GroveHR)
  • Optional: Reason for deletion

What Happens Next?

Once your request is received, we will process the account deletion within 7 business days. All associated data will be permanently removed unless we are required to retain it for legal or security reasons.

Thank you for using GroveHR.
