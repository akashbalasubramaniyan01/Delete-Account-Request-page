Delete Account Request

If you wish to request deletion of your account and associated personal data from the Employee Self Service (ESS) app, please follow the instructions below:

Steps to Request Deletion

  1. Send an email to akashkannan7139@gmail.com using your registered email ID.
  2. Include the subject line: "Account Deletion Request"
  3. Mention your full name and employee ID in the email body.

What Will Be Deleted

  • Personal profile information
  • Leave, OD, and attendance records
  • Sign-in/sign-out location history

Data Retention

Some data may be retained for legal or HR compliance purposes for up to 90 days after deletion. After that, it will be permanently removed.

For any questions, please contact akashkannan7139@gmail.com.
